What is trove?

Trove lets you store digital photos, videos, and audio in physical space. Leave an item, and the next person to visit that spot will be able to pick it up in exchange for leaving an item of their own (even money!)

How does it work?

We have created two versions of Trove.
The iOS app takes advantage of Bluetooth technology using Estimote Beacons. It connects an external server to a local beacon, turning the beacons into digital boxes.
The Android app uses GPS to create troves in specific spots, kind of like digital geocaching.

Estimote beacons let us alert you when you're near a trove.
